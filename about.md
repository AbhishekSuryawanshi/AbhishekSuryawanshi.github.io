# About Me
I am a **Senior iOS Developer with 7+ years of experience** designing and building high-quality mobile applications for the Apple ecosystem. My expertise lies in developing scalable and reliable applications using **Swift, SwiftUI, Objective-C**, and modern iOS frameworks.

Over the years, I have contributed to multiple production-grade applications published on the **App Store**, ranging from consumer apps to enterprise-level platforms. I particularly enjoy solving complex technical challenges such as **real-time communication systems, performance optimization, and scalable mobile architectures**.

My experience also includes implementing advanced features like **VoIP calling, WebRTC-based audio/video communication, push notification systems, and secure networking layers**.

I am passionate about building intuitive user experiences, writing clean and maintainable code, and continuously learning new technologies within the Apple ecosystem.
